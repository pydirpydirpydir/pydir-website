Who We Are
Our vision is simple.
PYDIR was born from a simple idea: playing is more fun when you can compete against others — and even better when there are real rewards at the end.

In our app, you compete against other players over a full month. The goal is to set your best time and climb the leaderboard. Those who rank near the top win real monthly rewards — vouchers are handed out in the first week of the new month, directly in the app.

Behind PYDIR stands a small team of developers, designers, and creative minds working together to build an app that's fun, fair, and motivates people to always give their best.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about PYDIR.

Is PYDIR really free — and do Free users actually win vouchers?

+
Yes, completely. Free users compete in their own leaderboard (top 10 win) and receive real vouchers every single month — no catch, no hidden fees. Premium unlocks unlimited play credits and bigger prizes — and Free players still win real rewards.

How exactly does the monthly competition work?

+
Every competition runs for one calendar month. You play, set your best time, and climb the leaderboard. At the end of the month the rankings lock. If you're in the top 10 of your leaderboard (Free or Premium), your voucher is automatically credited — no application needed.

What kind of vouchers can I win, and how much are they worth?

+
Real monetary vouchers — no conditions, no minimum spend, no brand restrictions. The exact value depends on your rank: the higher you finish, the bigger the reward. All current prize values are shown in the leaderboard table on this page. Best of all: you pick your own voucher, right in the PYDIR app. At the start of the month you choose your preferred voucher and can change it anytime until the end of the month. From the 1st of the new month the choice is locked — changes are then only possible by emailing support@pydir.com.

How do I know the competition is fair?

+
Free users only compete against Free users, and Premium users only against Premium users — no pay-to-win advantage. Rankings are based purely on in-game performance. We are a five-person team and our reputation is everything. Fairness is non-negotiable for us.

I have a question not answered here — who do I contact?

+
Use the contact form on this page. We respond within 24 hours — guaranteed. You can also reach us at support@pydir.com or on Discord.

How and when do I receive my voucher?

+
At the end of each month the rankings lock. If you finished in the top 10 of your leaderboard, your voucher is handed out during the first week of the new month — directly inside the PYDIR app. You'll receive a push notification as soon as it's available, and you can redeem it right away. No form, no application, no waiting for an email.

What happens when my credits run out?

+
Credits are what you use to enter races. Free players receive a fixed amount of credits each month; when they're used up, you can either buy more in-game or simply wait for the monthly reset, which refills your Coins & Credits automatically. Premium players (€1.99/month) play with unlimited credits and earn double in-game rewards.

When do the leaderboard and prizes reset?

+
Everything runs on a monthly cycle. The competition covers one calendar month, and at the start of each new month both the leaderboard and the prizes reset completely. Everyone starts from zero — even if it didn't work out last month, every new month is a fresh chance to win.

Is there a minimum age to play PYDIR?

+
PYDIR is recommended for players aged 13 and up. Players under 13 don't have access to social features and chat, and some features require a minimum age of 16. Full details for parents — including in-app purchases and screen-time tips — are in our Parental Guide in the footer of this page.

Is PYDIR really 100% ad-free — and how do you make money?

+
Yes — no banners, no pop-ups, no video ads, ever. PYDIR is funded by the optional Premium subscription (€1.99/month) and by in-game credit purchases. On top of that, we run a partner program: online retailers and shops cooperate with us, and through these partnerships we make additional vouchers available to our players — still with zero ads on your screen. We never sell your attention to advertisers, and playing for free stays completely possible.
